The Prompt

"Do you think university education should be free for everyone? Explain your reasoning."

Student's Essay

I firmly believe that university education should be completely free for everyone, regardless of their socioeconomic background. Education is a fundamental human right, not a privilege reserved for the wealthy. When education is commodified, we lose out on the immense potential of brilliant minds who simply cannot afford the exorbitant tuition fees.

Furthermore, a highly educated populace benefits society as a whole. It leads to greater innovation, higher economic stability, and a more informed democratic process. While some argue that free education would increase taxes, I believe the long-term return on investment far outweighs the initial costs.
